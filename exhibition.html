Kusama's Pop

Yayoi Kusama Museum, Shinjuku, Japan

Yayoi Kusama’s work, with its vivid colors and striking clarity, has often been regarded as “pop.” When tracing back, this characterization reveals intersections between her work and the Pop Art movement that flourished in New York in the 1960s, where she was then based.
